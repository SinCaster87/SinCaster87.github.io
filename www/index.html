By Gamers, For Gamers

We are committed to understanding how gamers think and behave. By removing personal and environmental obstables, we help each other achieve personal growth. While we are not a replacement for medical treatment or mental illness, we try our best to train people to coach each other into growth in a casual environment. The coaches we train understand gamers and their environment and are suited to help them improve their health.

Join Our Discord!

Our discord server is a safe space for anyone everyone. Our community in this server has over 41 thousand people. We have lots of discussion chats for helping people with mental health. Chats span from school life, to grief and loss, to general mental health and improvement. While this is not replacement for professional help, we believe that having peers to discuss and grow alongside is integral to a gamers health improvement.

Mental Health

We offer several channels to discuss with peers on things that are affecting your mental health. Things for example like you have lots of self doubt or you are just having a lot of problems and do not know how to fix it. You can talk about it with others in chats, and discuss things you have in common and perhaps find new ways to discover methods to help yourself improve your mental health through our community.

Relationships

One big part of how your mental health is affected is by having an unhealthy relationship. Whether it is a love based one, with your parents, or with your friends. It you are going to be happier in life, you have to be happier with interacting with the people that you talk to.

Confidence

Believing in yourself is step one to moving forward. If you have probelms with this, there is no shame in stepping forward and talking about it with your peers.

Grief and Loss

Everyone experiences losing someone close to them. Whether is was by death or they just are not close to that person anymore, talking about your experience with other people can be a lucrative way to find support and comfort. While you might not feel better or be able to get past it, we believe that having others with you will make the suffering much less difficult to geth through.

The Man Behind it All

Dr. Alok Kanojia, a psychiatrist that goes by Dr. K, is the poster-boy and the man behind our organization. Dr. K ususally broadcasts conversations that he holds with popular gamers and streamers. he also holds lectures about various mental health improvement techniques. Dr. K does talk with a limited amount of people, but since he averages tens of thousands of viewers, countless young adults are being exposed to healthy discussions about mental health and can apply what they took away from it to themselves. They are also able to see the benefits of seeking help.
